### The Problem
Blackthorn Geomatics needed a website that looked credible alongside industry leaders like Trimble and SAM — professional, fast, and easy to navigate for clients evaluating surveying services.

### What I Built
I went on-site to shoot photo and video of their operations, then built a Next.js site featuring custom carousels, inline video, and downloadable PDF resources. The priority was making complex geospatial services easy to parse for non-technical visitors while maintaining the visual credibility expected in the surveying industry.

### Stack
Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, Vercel
