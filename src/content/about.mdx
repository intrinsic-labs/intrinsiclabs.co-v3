### Who I Am

I'm a developer and designer building software that's grounded in clarity, craft, and real-world usefulness. Intrinsic Labs is where that work lives — a solo studio focused on shipping products and tools that hold up under scrutiny.

### How I Work

I care about architecture. Not in the abstract, but in the way good structure makes everything downstream — iteration, debugging, onboarding, scaling — dramatically easier. I think in systems, and I build things that reflect that.

Most of my work spans the full stack: from UI design and frontend implementation through API design, data modeling, and infrastructure. I'm most at home in TypeScript and React ecosystems, but I pick tools based on the problem, not habit.

### What I'm Focused On

Right now I'm building [Aspen Grove](/case-studies/aspen-grove), an exploration-first AI interface, and shipping client work that ranges from multilingual fitness platforms to operational software for physical environments.

I'm always interested in hard problems, good conversation, and work that matters. If that sounds like your thing, [get in touch](mailto:helloworld@intrinsiclabs.co).
