### The Problem
My client runs a women's Bible study ministry and needed a website where they could sell study materials, publish blog content, and manage everything themselves. They needed a real shopping experience — not just a Shopify embed — but also needed to be able to update content without calling a developer.

### What I Built
Full e-commerce implementation with custom UI: product listings, cart, checkout via Stripe, and email order reporting to the client. Blog backed by Sanity CMS with webhook-triggered revalidation on publish — the client updates content in Sanity and the site rebuilds automatically.

I also went on-site to shoot b-roll footage that serves as the hero video, along with product photography for the online store.

The entire site is backed by Sanity so the client has full content ownership. Fast load times were a priority — optimized images, minimal JavaScript, edge deployment.

### Stack
Next.js, Sanity CMS, Stripe, Tailwind CSS, Vercel

### Status
Live and in production. Client manages all content independently.
